# Introduction

Welcome to Team Plot Twist's group project for assignment 3 of 
WPI's CS573 Data Visualization course.
<br><br>
In this survey, you will be shown a series of charts.
<br><br>
For each chart, you will be asked to estimate the percentage of one chart element to another.
<br><br>
In the bar charts, one bar will be marked with a dot, and another bar will be marked with a star.
<br>
In the pie charts, one pie slice will be marked with a dot, and another pie slice will be marked with a star.
<br>
In the mosaic charts, one rectangular section will be marked with a dot, and another section will be marked with a star.
<br><br>
No personally identifying information is collected as part of this study. We will be aggregating the response data to create visualizations for a class assignment.
<br><br>
Thank you for taking the time to participate in our survey!
